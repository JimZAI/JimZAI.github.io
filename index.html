Ji Zhang

I'm currently a fourth-year Ph.D student at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), where I'm very fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Jingkuan Song and Prof. Lianli Gao.

My research mainly focuses on Few-Shot Learning (FSL), Out-of-distribution (OOD) Detection and Continue Learning. I'm now especially interested in developing advanced algorithms that enable rapid adaptation of large pre-trained vision and language models, e.g. via Prompt Tuning. If you are also interested in related topics, please do not hesitate to reach out.
